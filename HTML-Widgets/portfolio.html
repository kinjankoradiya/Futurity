Investment manager

Vanguard Australian Fixed Interest Index Fund

strategy

Sector

Style

Passive

Risk

9

Investment Objective

To provide a return (after fees and expenses and before taxes) which exceeds the S&P/ASX 300 Accumulation Index over rolling four-year periods.

Investment Strategy

The opportunity to invest in a diversified share portfolio of companies predominately listed on the ASX and selected on the basis of their social, enviromental and financial credentials. The fund utlises an active stock-picking management style with stock generally selected for growth rather than income, with a bias towards smaller capitalisation stock listed on the ASX. All stocks are chosen on the basis of relative value where we deem the risk are being adequately priced.
